Get Involved
Join the Work

Support Black Wellness Project LA

There are many ways to show up for this work. Find what fits you — every form of support makes direct community health programming possible.

01

Volunteer

Show up at our events. No medical background required — just your time, your energy, and your care for community. Volunteers are the backbone of everything we do.

We welcome students, community members, healthcare professionals, and everyone in between.

Sign Up to Volunteer

What volunteers do:

  • Help run health screening stations
  • Staff registration and welcome tables
  • Support wellness activity stations
  • Assist with kids’ corner activities
  • Help with setup and breakdown
  • Conduct outreach and canvassing before events
02

Donate

Every dollar we receive goes directly to programming — supplies for health screenings, food at events, childcare access, transportation for community members, and the vendors who make our events possible.

We are a grassroots, student-run initiative. Your donation sustains this work.

Donate to BWP-LA

Your donation supports:

  • Medical supplies and screening equipment
  • Food and refreshments at community events
  • Childcare for attending families
  • Transportation assistance for participants
  • Venue and logistics costs
  • Resource navigation materials
03

Become a Partner

We’re always looking for community organizations, clinics, advocacy groups, and institutions to build with. Partnerships that are rooted in shared values create outcomes that outlast any single event.

If your organization is aligned with our mission of improving Black health outcomes in Los Angeles, let’s talk.

Start a Conversation

Current partners:

  • TRAP Medicine
  • UCLA Health
  • Charles R. Drew Medical Society
  • Seeing Over Large Obstacles (SOLO)
  • UCLA Center of Excellence
  • Elevated Hands (Black-owned wellness)
04

Sponsor an Event

Sponsoring a specific event or initiative allows your organization to directly support a day of free community health services. It’s targeted, meaningful, and visible.

We can work with sponsors at any level — from covering costs for a single screening station to becoming a title sponsor of our annual health fair.

Inquire About Sponsorship

Sponsorship opportunities include:

  • Title sponsor of the Annual Community Health Fair
  • Health screening station sponsor
  • Wellness activity sponsor (yoga, sound bath, massage)
  • Kids’ corner sponsor
  • Food and refreshments sponsor
  • Event supplies and equipment sponsor
