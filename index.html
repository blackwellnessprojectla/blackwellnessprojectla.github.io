Advancing Black Health Through Community, Care, and Future Physicians

Black Wellness Project LA is a physician-led community initiative focused on building access, trust, and continuity in Black health. We partner with local organizations to deliver health education, navigation support, and community events.

Mission

To advance Black health in Los Angeles by connecting community members to education, resources, and care, while building a future physician workforce grounded in service, trust, and accountability.

What we do

Community health events

Pop-ups and fairs focused on prevention, screenings, and practical health navigation.

Health education

Short, accessible content that meets people where they are, online and in person.

Resource navigation

Guidance on connecting with clinics, insurance resources, and follow-up care.
