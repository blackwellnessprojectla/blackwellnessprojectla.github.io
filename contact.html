Common Questions

I’m a community member. How do I find out about your events?

Email us to get on our list, or follow us on social media. All events are free and open to the public. We also do direct outreach in the Baldwin Village area before each event.

I’m a medical student. Can I get involved?

Yes! We’re always welcoming new student members, especially from UCLA, CDU, and UCI — though students from any school are welcome to volunteer. Reach out by email.

I’m a healthcare provider who wants to offer services at your fair. How does that work?

We work with healthcare providers and wellness practitioners to staff our health fair. Email us with details about your practice and what services you’d like to offer.

Are your events open to everyone?

Yes. All of our events are free and open to the community. Our health fair is especially designed for and centered on Black residents of South Los Angeles, and everyone is welcome.
