Free Event

Community Listening Sessions

📍 Baldwin Village Area, Los Angeles  |  Dates to be announced

Before we plan, we listen. Our community listening sessions bring us into direct, unhurried conversation with Black residents of Los Angeles to understand health needs, barriers, and priorities from the people who know them best.

Sessions are small, facilitated, and designed to be comfortable and accessible. No medical knowledge required — just your voice and your experience.

  • Small group format with trained student facilitators
  • Open to all residents of the Baldwin Village area
  • Childcare and light refreshments provided
  • Accessible venue — transportation support available
  • Your input directly shapes our 2026 health fair programming

Free Event

Health Education Workshops

📍 Los Angeles, CA  |  Dates to be announced

Our health education workshops are designed in direct response to what community members tell us they want to learn. We don’t show up with a predetermined curriculum — we listen first, then build together.

Workshops are interactive, accessible, and led by medical students and community educators who reflect the communities we serve.

  • Topics chosen based on community listening session findings
  • Interactive, not lecture-based
  • Resource handouts and take-home tools provided
  • Light refreshments and welcoming space
  • Minimum of 2 workshops planned for Spring 2026

Flagship Event

Annual Community Health Fair

📍 Greater Baldwin Village, Los Angeles  |  Mid-August 2026  |  Full day event

Our flagship event returns. The Annual Community Health Fair is a full-day celebration of health, community, and care — built on our Mind, Body & Soul framework and shaped by everything we learn from the community throughout the year.

All services are completely free. All are welcome. This is for our community, built by people who come from it.

  • Free health screenings: blood pressure, blood glucose, oral cancer, PSA, STI/HIV testing, and more
  • Wellness activities: yoga, sound bath, healing circles, massage
  • Social needs assessments and resource navigation
  • Kids’ corner with activities and photo booth
  • Music, food, and community celebration
  • Trusted community partners on-site

Inaugural Event

Dr. Edith Mitchell Community Health Fair

📍 Baldwin Village, South Los Angeles  |  Summer 2024

Our inaugural event — named in honor of pioneering oncologist Dr. Edith Mitchell — served over 120 residents of Baldwin Village with free health screenings, wellness activities, social needs assessments, and community celebration.

  • 120+ residents served
  • 100+ health screenings conducted (blood pressure, glucose, cancer, PSA, STI/HIV)
  • 50%+ of attendees participated in at least 2 wellness activities
  • 24 social needs assessments; 16 individuals successfully connected to resources
  • Partners: TRAP Medicine, UCLA Health, Elevated Hands, SOLO, and more
